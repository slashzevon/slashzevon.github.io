Church of Flesh

The Four Tenets of Membership:
  • 1. Belief in a higher power, Creator of our universe; provider of ultimate free will.
  • 2. Love, of ourselves and then others, is the guiding force of God.
  • 3. We hold authority over our flesh; no man can order us to consume anything, in any way, against our wish.
  • 4. We use our God given senses, sight, touch, taste, smell, hearing, and our inner self, in order to discriminate information; NOT the words of other men, or the vehicles of technology. To do otherwise would be heresy.
